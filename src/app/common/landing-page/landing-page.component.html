FAQ for Kuwait

FAQ(Frequently Asked Questions)

Question 1. How do I know if my Visa Application has been submitted to the Company or Sponsor and the current status of the visa application?
Answer: Contact the Company or Sponsor or visit https://eservices-visacheck-kw.online and provide your Passport Number, Date of Birth and Nationality information.

Question 2. How do I know that the Visa issued in My Name is correct?
Answer: You can visit https://eservices-visacheck-kw.online and download the Visa issued in Your Name by providing your Passport Number, Date of Birth and Nationality information.

Question 3. How many days to travel to Kuwait after the Employment Visa is issued?
Answer: The Visa contains the date of issue and expiry. It is Mandatory for the Visa Holder to arrive in Kuwait before the expiry date.

Question 4. Is the Salary written on the Employment Visa?
Answer: No, Salary is not recorded. However, the Approval Copy contains the Salary which is provided by the Ministry of Interior to the Company or Sponsor at the time of Visa issuance.

Question 5. Can I work other than the Profession that is written on my Visa? Or whether the Company or Sponsor can give me other types of work outside of my Profession?
Answer: There is no such opportunity legally in Kuwait. The Company or Sponsor cannot engage in Occupations other than those specified in the Visa

Question 6. What is the difference between Manual and e-Visa?
Answer: There is no difference, Manual Visa is issued manually and e-Visa is issued electronically

Question 7. Are Manual visa and e-Visa numbers the same?
Answer: e-Visa Guidelines: (A) Visa number starting with 2 and totaling 9 digits (B) 1st MOI Reference number starting with 4 or 3 and totaling 9 digits & (C) 2nd MOI Reference number starting with any of number and totaling 6 digits.

Question 8. Can the Company or Sponsor cancel the newly issued Visa when I live in my country?
Answer: No one can cancel the Visa before the expiry date.
